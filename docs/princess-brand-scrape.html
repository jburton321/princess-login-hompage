03 — SVG Brand Shapes
Logo Mark and Graphic Elements
Reconstructed SVG approximations of key brand marks based on the brand book and site assets.

Sea Witch Logo Mark

The "Our Princess" — a woman's silhouette with flowing hair becoming ocean waves, crowned with a tiara. The central brand icon, used on hulls and standalone branding.

Wave Line Motif

Signature horizontal wave pattern derived from the sea witch's flowing hair. Used as section dividers, background texture, and decorative accents across print and digital.

Tiara Crown

The crown element isolated from the sea witch mark. Appears in gold for premium tiers (Sanctuary Collection, Suites) and in blue for standard branding contexts.

MedallionClass Badge

Circular badge form used for the MedallionClass wearable tech branding. Double-ring motif with centered type. Used in icons, app UI, and on-ship signage.

Ship Profile

Stylized cruise ship silhouette used in destination cards, itinerary graphics, and promotional materials. The sea witch appears prominently on the hull of all vessels.

Compass Rose

Navigational motif used in destination exploration contexts, itinerary maps, and wayfinding graphics. Reflects the brand's explorer heritage.

05 — Brand Identity
Positioning and Personality
Key brand strategy extracted from the official PCL Brand Book (Feb 2020).

Archetype

The Everyperson — relatable, inclusive, down-to-earth. Not elitist. A quiet leader who is hard-working, collaborative, irresistibly likable and empathetic without being touchy-feely.

Brand Promise

Help you connect to what matters most — your loved ones, the destinations you visit, and new friends.

Shared Purpose

Share our world, share our hearts, protect our Earth, and create lasting memories.

Voice Persona

"Charming Fellow Traveler and Friend" — happy, relatable, well-traveled but never a bore. Contagious enthusiasm without being annoying. A glass of wine away from sassy.

Target Audience

The Meaningful Traveler — seeks connection with loved ones, cultures, and shared experiences. Well-balanced, people-people who know the world is better experienced together.

Brand Qualities

Genuine, Engaging, Innovative, Happy, Passionate, Expert. Copy should be grounded and relatable, not elitist or overly hip.

Copy Don'ts

No clichés, no puns, no cutesy language, no long feature lists, no flowery/dramatic prose. Write simply so it can be appreciated by all.

Sea Witch Identity

"Our Princess is not some mythological sea creature or aloof member of royalty. She's real and relatable." Her hair represents connection — a ripple turning into a wave.

08 — UI Patterns
Component Observations
Key UI patterns and design decisions observed across the live site.

Border Radius

Cards use 8-12px radius. Buttons use 4-6px. No fully rounded pills on primary CTAs — they stay rectangular with slight rounding.

Card Shadows

Light and subtle. Typically 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08). No heavy drop shadows. Elevation is communicated through background color shifts rather than shadows.

CTA Buttons

Solid Princess Blue (#00538B) background with white text. No outlines or ghosts for primary CTA. Secondary CTAs use outlined/bordered style with blue text.

Carousel Pattern

Horizontal scroll carousels are the primary content pattern. Slide counter shows "01 / 06" format. Auto-advance with manual navigation dots and arrows.

Image Treatment

Scene7 CDN with aspect ratio modifiers (16x9, 4x5, 1x1). Images are crisp, warm, interpersonal — showing connection between people rather than solo travelers. Lighting is soft and natural.

Platform

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) as CMS. Adobe Dynamic Media (Scene7) for image delivery. Custom component library with horizontal scroll patterns prominent throughout.
